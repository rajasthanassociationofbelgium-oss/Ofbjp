About OFBJP Belgium

Overseas Friends of BJP Belgium (OFBJP Belgium) is a voluntary, non-profit community platform dedicated to strengthening the bonds between the Indian diaspora and Belgium while promoting the values of service, cultural engagement and community participation.

Guided by the timeless Indian philosophy of वसुधैव कुटुम्बकम् (Vasudhaiva Kutumbakam) — "The World is One Family" — OFBJP Belgium serves as a bridge connecting people, cultures and ideas. Our members come from diverse professional, cultural and regional backgrounds, united by a shared commitment to community service and constructive engagement.

OFBJP Belgium supports the vision of a strong, prosperous and globally connected India while fostering friendship and cooperation between India and Belgium. Through cultural events, community initiatives, educational activities, social outreach programmes and public engagement, we encourage meaningful participation of the Indian diaspora in Belgian society.

Our organisation is powered entirely by volunteers who dedicate their time, energy and expertise to serve the community. We believe in integrity, inclusiveness, respect, responsible leadership and the spirit of selfless service.

Our mission is to create a vibrant and united Indian community in Belgium, promote cultural exchange, support social initiatives and strengthen the enduring friendship between India and Belgium for future generations.
