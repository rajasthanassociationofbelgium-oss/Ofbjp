About OFBJP Belgium

Overseas Friends of BJP Belgium serves as a platform for members of the Indian diaspora to connect, engage, celebrate Indian culture and strengthen ties between India and Belgium.

We encourage community participation, cultural exchange and constructive dialogue among Indians living across Belgium.
